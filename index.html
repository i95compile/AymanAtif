Frequently Asked Questions
Clear answers, no surprises

These are the questions I’m asked most often before starting an SEO project. If something isn’t covered here, feel free to reach out directly.

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is not instant. In most cases, early signals appear within 6–8 weeks, while meaningful traffic and ranking improvements typically happen over 3–6 months. Long-term, SEO compounds and becomes one of the most stable acquisition channels.

Do you guarantee rankings or traffic?

No - and anyone who does is not being honest. What I do guarantee is a clean process, best-practice execution, transparent reporting, and decisions based on data and search intent, not guesses.

Is this technical SEO, content SEO, or both?

Both. Technical SEO creates the foundation, while content and on-page optimization drive growth. I focus on aligning infrastructure, content, and intent so everything works together instead of in isolation.

Will this work for my type of business?

SEO works best for businesses with clear search demand and a long-term growth mindset. During the audit phase, I assess whether SEO makes sense for your specific market before recommending next steps.

How much does SEO cost?

Pricing depends on your website size, competition level, and goals. I don’t offer one-size-fits-all packages because SEO strategies should be tailored, not recycled.

Most projects typically fall within a monthly range after an initial audit. After reviewing your site, I’ll provide a clear scope, priorities, and pricing - with no hidden costs or long-term lock-ins.

Do you work on existing sites or only new ones?

Both. I often work on existing websites that need cleanup, restructuring, or strategic direction. New sites benefit from building SEO correctly from day one.

What do you need from me to get started?

Access to analytics tools (if available), a basic understanding of your business goals, and openness to collaboration. I handle the technical and strategic heavy lifting.
